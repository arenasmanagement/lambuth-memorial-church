About Us

You Belong Here

A place to start again.

Come as you are. We’d love to meet you.

Whether you’ve been in church your whole life or haven’t stepped into one in years, you are welcome here. No pressure. No expectations. Just a place where you can come as you are and take a step forward.

Lambuth Memorial United Methodist Church is a congregation in the heart of Jackson, Tennessee. We gather each Sunday for worship at 10:15 AM, and every other Saturday for Bible study and basketball. However you find us, we hope you’ll find a home.
