About Us

You Belong Here

A place to start again.

Come as you are. We’d love to meet you.

Whether you’ve been in church your whole life or haven’t stepped into one in years, you are welcome here. No pressure. No expectations. Just a place where you can come as you are and take a step forward.

Our Mission

Lambuth Memorial United Methodist Church exists to welcome people as they are, help them grow in faith, and spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ through worship, community, and service.

We’re a congregation in the heart of Jackson, Tennessee. We gather each Sunday for worship at 10:15 AM, and every other Saturday for Bible study and basketball. However you find us, we hope you’ll find a home.

Bible Study and Basketball are held every other Saturday. Please check our social media for the next scheduled date.

Meet Our Pastor

Mike Peery

Lay Pastor

Welcome — I’m so glad you found us. Whether you’re just curious, returning after a long time away, or looking for a church home, there’s a place for you here at Lambuth Memorial.

A little about me: I’m married to Denise, and while we don’t have any children, we do have two dogs, two cats, and a big backyard — thank heavens!

I’ve played music for over 50 years — rock, country, and gospel. Accounting and music were my background, but the Lord called me to serve as Lay Pastor here at Lambuth Memorial. This is the honor of my life.

“Jesus Christ is perfect. Our Holy Bible is perfect. You’ll hear about both if you visit us at 10:15.”

We would love to have you worship with us. Come as you are — I hope to see you this Sunday.

God bless,
— Pastor Mike
